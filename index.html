My Lore

Every developer embarks on a journey of learning and growth. Here are the key quests that have shaped my path as a full-stack JavaScript developer.

My journey into software development began with a spark—video games, especially those created by Nintendo. Their creativity and innovation inspired me to explore programming and learn how to bring ideas to life through code.

Now, I’m working toward becoming a full-stack JavaScript developer, focusing on building dynamic interfaces with React and robust backends with Node.js. This portfolio is where I share my progress, showcase my skills, and demonstrate my passion for crafting complete, functional applications.

    My journey into software development began with a love for video games. From the moment I first held a controller, I knew I wanted to create worlds just as magical. Driven by curiosity, I started learning basic programming concepts, beginning with Java, the language that introduced me to the craft of coding.

This period taught me resilience, adaptability, and the value of continuous growth as I worked toward landing a full-time role in the tech industry.
